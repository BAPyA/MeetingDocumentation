# BAPyA Board Elections

## 1. Self-Nominations

The board seeks nominations via a form such as a Google form:
https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1L6HIJ_giJ-QE4riIX4CsI1NZ5LX-9rcW57OzQU6R6cY/edit

> **BAPyA (Bay Area Python Association) is seeking board nominations for the 2025 election cycle**
>
> We are seeking self-nominations from individuals who are active in the SF Bay Area Python community and are willing to serve a 2 year term on the BAPyA board.
>
> Responses are needed by **February 28, 2025** at midnight PST.
> All nominated individuals will be notified by March 15, 2025.

## 2. Responses/Candidates

The self-nomination responses are saved on spreadsheet connected to the form such as Google Sheets.

## 3. Dates

The open and close date must be decided by the board.

## 6. Method of Voting

- Approval voting (pick 1 or more acceptable candidates)
- Runoff vote for ties

## 7. Results

- Final vote results
